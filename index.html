How much protein do you need to actually build muscle?

  • 1

    According to the most recent research and established meta-analyses, 0.8g of protein per pound of body weight is often cited as the "sweet spot" or the "plateau point" for muscle growth.

  • 2

    The Optimal Range: Most research indicates that muscle protein synthesis is maximized at around 0.7g to 1.0g per pound of body weight. Example: If you weigh 100 lbs, it is recommended you take 70g to 100g of protein daily.
